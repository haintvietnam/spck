UEFA Congress: Date, location and agenda

Published: April 2, 2025 By: UEFA Media News

The 49th Ordinary UEFA Congress will take place in Belgrade, Serbia on Thursday 8 April 2025. The Serbian capital will welcome representatives from all 55 UEFA member associations for this important annual gathering of European football's governing body.

Congress Agenda

The Congress will address several key topics, including:

  • Presidential address and activity report
  • Financial statements and budget
  • Updates on UEFA competitions
  • Strategic planning for 2025-2030
  • Updates on Football Development programs
"The UEFA Congress is a vital opportunity for all of European football to come together, discuss our shared challenges and opportunities, and chart a course for the future of our sport." - UEFA President

Belgrade: A Historic Host

Belgrade has a rich football history and has previously hosted several major UEFA events. The Serbian Football Association, founded in 1919, is one of the oldest in Europe and has been instrumental in developing the sport in the region.

Key Issues

Among the key issues expected to be discussed are the continued development of women's football, sustainability initiatives in football administration and events, and the ongoing evolution of UEFA club competitions.

The Congress will also include presentations on UEFA's financial health and investment in grassroots football across Europe.

Practical Information

The Congress is scheduled to begin at 10:00 CET and will be streamed live on UEFA.tv. Media accreditation details will be announced in the coming weeks.
